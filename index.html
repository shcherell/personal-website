About Me

Hi, I'm Elena Shcherbinina. Welcome to my personal website! Here you'll find more about my journey, my work, and my passion for transforming ideas into action.

I am a project manager with 30 years of experience. I'm 37 and I've been a project manager since childhood, though I didn't realize it at the time. Every project I encounter is immediately broken down in my mind into tasks. I assess all necessary resources, budget, and timelines, while simultaneously evaluating risks and developing mitigation strategies. After each project, I conduct a thorough review to extract valuable lessons learned.

Whether it's a move, a children's birthday party, developing a new marketing product for a million-strong audience, or a trip to the store, I always fully immerse myself in the project and understand all its details. I believe that only through such dedication can a project manager perform their job effectively.

My team holds a special place in my heart. I see myself not as a leader, but as a facilitator who helps the team excel, motivates them, assists in overcoming obstacles, finds necessary resources, and resolves conflicts. Everything is done to achieve the best possible results.
